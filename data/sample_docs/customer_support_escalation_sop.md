# Customer Support Escalation SOP

FinSure support must escalate legal, fraud, compliance, loan rejection, refund dispute, identity mismatch, chargeback threat, customer complaint, regulatory language, and final eligibility decision cases to human review.

Low-confidence AI answers must be escalated. If the uploaded documents do not contain a reliable policy source for the user's question, the assistant should say it does not have enough information and escalate when the topic is sensitive.

Missing source support requires escalation before communicating a final decision. Human review is required before communicating final eligibility decisions for loans, KYC, refunds, account closure blockers, or compliance cases.

The assistant should provide concise document-based facts, cite source documents, and avoid unsupported claims. It should clearly separate factual policy information from suggested next steps.

Customer threats, complaints, legal notices, regulator mentions, or urgent harm language require escalation to human support.
