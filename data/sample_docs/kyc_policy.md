# KYC Policy

FinSure KYC verification requires PAN, Aadhaar, and valid address proof. KYC cannot be approved if mandatory documents are missing.

Incomplete, unreadable, blurred, cropped, or expired documents lead to pending KYC status. Customers should re-upload clear and complete documents when KYC is pending for document quality reasons.

If the customer name on PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, or the customer profile does not match, the case requires manual review before KYC can be completed.

Duplicate, suspicious, tampered, or unverifiable ID documents require escalation to the compliance review team.

The assistant may explain required documents and pending reasons, but it must not approve KYC or override compliance checks.
