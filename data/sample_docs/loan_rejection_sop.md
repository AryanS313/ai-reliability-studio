# Loan Rejection SOP

Common FinSure loan rejection reasons include low credit score, insufficient income proof, failed KYC, high existing debt, inconsistent application information, incomplete application details, and policy risk flags.

The assistant must not manually approve loans, reverse rejection outcomes, promise approval, or disclose internal credit scoring thresholds.

Reconsideration requests, manual approval requests, rejection disputes, and exception requests must be escalated to the credit review team.

Final loan eligibility decisions require human review. The assistant may explain general rejection reasons when grounded in this SOP, but it must not communicate a final eligibility decision.

Customers may be advised to update income proof, complete KYC, correct inconsistent application information, and wait for credit review when a reconsideration request is escalated.
