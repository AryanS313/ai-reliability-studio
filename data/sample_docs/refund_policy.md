# Refund Policy

FinSure refunds are allowed only within 7 days of the original transaction date when the payment was completed successfully and the customer provides a valid transaction ID.

Refund requests after 7 days require support review before a decision can be communicated. Refunds after 30 days are generally not eligible unless fraud is suspected.

Fraud-related refund claims must be escalated to human support review. Chargeback threats, refund disputes, duplicate payment refund requests, and customer complaints about a denied refund also require support review.

Duplicate payment refund requests require transaction verification before any refund decision. Refund decisions must cite the transaction ID and the policy reason used for the decision.

The assistant may explain refund policy facts from this document, but it must not promise that a refund will be approved.
